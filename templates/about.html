About TextUtils

TextUtils is a simple web application that helps you quickly modify and analyze your text.

What You Can Do

  • Remove punctuations from text
  • Convert text to UPPERCASE
  • Remove extra spaces
  • Remove new lines

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide an easy and fast tool for students, developers, and writers to clean and format their text instantly.

Developer

This project is built using Django and Bootstrap to demonstrate simple text processing utilities in a web application.
